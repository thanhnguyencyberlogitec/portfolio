Slant

Project information

  • Category: Blog
  • Client: Dafe Ojaide
  • Skills: Backbone.js, Node.js, Firebase
  • Project URL: https://www.slant.co

A media platform to gauge public opinion on topics ranging from politics to film. I developed and deployed a full-stack application in Backbone.js, Node.js, and MySQL to AWS. Its features include email authentication, customizable profiles, content search, posts, comments, likes, polls, notifications, inbox messaging, and account settings. I was also heavily involved in customer research and iterating on the product based on feedback.
